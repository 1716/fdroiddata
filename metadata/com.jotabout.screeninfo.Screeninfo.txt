Summary:Display your screen configuration parameters
Description:
Shows the screen configuration for an Android device, including the screen size (small, normal, large, xlarge) and the density classification (ldpi, mpdi, hdpi, xhdpi). Also shows other screen metrics, as well as the calculated screen size in physical units. Users can email the report or share it to other apps such as Dropbox, etc.

This information is useful for developers producing layouts for their Android applications, or to assist developers in customer support scenarios.
