Description:
Comprehensive music player with unusual features such as: sharing files over Android Beam, now-playing to 
Twitter and automatic playback on headphone insertion. By installing the optional Podcast Provider and/or 
Ampache Provider (also in the F-droid app repo) you can listen to audio from those sources. For example, you 
can store your music on an Owncloud instance and stream it to Just-Player.

This version of Just-Player requires  Android 3.0+
