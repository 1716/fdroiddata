Summary:Enhanced TTS plugin for FBReader
Description:
Set rate , pitch, volume, language. Pause at the end of sentences. Better Bluetooth support that can
control the app from another app. Just choose Speak+ from the FBReader context menu to activate.

The internet permission exists to enable the sending of crash logs to the developer. In the event of
a crash a dialogue will be displayed requesting your permission to send the logs.
